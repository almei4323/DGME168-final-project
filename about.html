I like travel and photography. I usually traveled internationally for eight weeks straight with just my carry-on duffle bag and a small camera bag. This picture of my carry-on luggage was taken at the ShangHai Airport. Once I got somewhere I liked, I would often leave my carry-on luggage in that city and take off on a week long trip to another city, town or scenic spot with just my camera bag or a cheap shoulder bag that I buy locally. The shoulder bag in this picture was purchased in Yichang, China for the purpose of traveling around Guangxi province for a week. It was only 70 yuan or about $10 U.S. money.
